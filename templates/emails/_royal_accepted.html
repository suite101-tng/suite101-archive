Congratulations – you've been accepted into Suite's royalties program. You can track your balance and payments on your stats page at stats/royalties.

We created this program as a partnership between Suite and people like you, the writers who help make our platform special. We're grateful you've decided to take part.

For every dollar we earn, 70¢ is set aside to pay royalties. Your own share is derived from your share of Suite's overall readership. (If your stories are responsible for 10% of the reads, you get 10% of the pot.)

Visit our support center to learn more, and feel free to get in touch if you have any questions at all.
